Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Our AI Chatbot application collects the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address for account creation and authentication
  • Google Account Data: If you sign in with Google, we receive your email and basic profile information
  • Chat Data: Your conversations with the AI assistant to provide chat history
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the application to improve our services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the chatbot service
  • Authenticate your account and manage access
  • Store and retrieve your chat history
  • Improve and optimize the application
  • Send important service-related notifications (OTP codes, etc.)

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your information.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: For AI-powered responses
  • Google OAuth 2.0: For secure Google Sign-In authentication
  • Email Service (Gmail SMTP): For sending OTP verification codes
  • MongoDB Atlas: For secure data storage

5. Data Retention

We retain your chat history and account information until you choose to delete it or close your account. You can delete individual chats or your entire account at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your chats and account
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Revoke Google Sign-In access at any time

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to maintain your session (JWT tokens) and improve user experience. No tracking cookies are used.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the application or via our GitHub repository.
